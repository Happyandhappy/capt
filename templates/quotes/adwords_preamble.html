Welcome to Captivise

Thank you for considering Captivise. There are 3 simple and easy steps to complete before you can gain access to Captivise.

Step 1. Conversion Tracking Checks

The next step in the process is for Captivise to connect to your account and check that you have tracking in place. Captivise connects seamlessly to your AdWords and Analytics to look for conversion tracking.

Once you've connected your account, the following page will show you which conversion tracking methods have not been set up (if any) and provide instructions on how to do so (if required).

If everything is working, you'll see 3 green ticks.

Step 2. Get an Instant Pricing Quote

Following the instant conversion tracking checks, Captivise will then analyse your spend for the most recent 30 days and provide you with a quote for how much the service would cost you if you spent the same again next month.

Step 3. Enter your Payment Details

If you are happy with your quote, click next and you will be asked for payment details. If you have any questions about your quote, please feel free to send a message via email or one of our customer support channels.

You won't be charged anything for the first 30 days if your fees are under £500 (i.e if your spend is under £10,000 for the most recent 30 days). Fees will be charged at £500 intervals just as with Google AdWords, for example, you will be charged £500 every time your account spend accrues a further £10,000 through the system.

You are done and ready to use Captivise

Once the quick and easy 3 step process is completed, you'll be up and running and ready to go. All you'll need to do is to select the campaigns that you wish to run Captivise on, decide whether to input margin percentage or target cost-per-conversion and then click 'go'.

You'll need to make sure your Adwords account is a manager account in order to use Captivise. If you don't have another choice, and/or don't know what a manager account is, please refer to this Google help article, for instructions on how to set one up.

Step 3. Enter your Payment Details

On the next page you will be required to enter your payment details. You will be redirected to the secure payment gateway, which will verify your card details, and allow billing in future.

You will not be charged at this point, nor at any point in the first 30 days if your calculated fees are under £500 (i.e if your spend is under £10,000 for the most recent 30days).
